## Based on filename

Use the naming convention of 2020-02-25-1582669093 (date-timestamp)

- Seach on date:
    *  `vim 2020-02-25`
- In case of multiple entries on the same date, use (sections of) the unique timestamp
    * `vim *093.md`
    * `vim *909*`
